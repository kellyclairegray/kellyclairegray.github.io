Rosalind Franklin

Made a crucial contribution in the discovery of the structure of DNA. She discovered the density of DNA, establishing that it existed in a helical confromation. Franklin also gave new insight on the structure of viruses, laying the foundation for the field of structural virology.

Lise Meitner

Was apart of the team that discovered nuclear fission. The team also discovered the radioactive element protactinium.

3/3
Katherine Johnson

Worked on the trajectory of space flight. Johnson calculated the trajectories that led Apollo 11 to the Moon. She also calculated the path for Freedom 7 which put the first U.S astronaut in space.
